### Achievements in Week 1:

* **Regarding Working Culture & Regulations:**
  * Fully understood and committed to complying with the working hours, system resource confidentiality policies, progress reporting workflows, and professional working style at the internship unit.

* **Regarding AWS Account & Budgets:**
  * Successfully registered and activated a personal AWS Free Tier account.
  * Successfully completed the rewarding tasks.
  * Successfully configured **AWS Budgets** with a realistic threshold (automatically sending email alerts when actual or forecasted costs exceed $1, or when reaching 80% of the Free Tier limit) to effectively manage costs throughout the learning and internship process.

* **Regarding AWS Support:**
  * Deeply understood the differences in benefits, response times, and costs between AWS Support Plans (Basic, Developer, Business, Enterprise).
  * Mastered using the AWS Support Center interface to submit technical support tickets or request Service Quotas increases when necessary.

* **Regarding AWS IAM (Identity and Access Management):**
  * Grasped the fundamental operations of IAM and the principle of **Least Privilege Access**.
  * Clearly distinguished between **IAM User** (identity for a specific person), **IAM Group** (a collection of users with shared permissions), **IAM Role** (temporary identity delegation for users or AWS services), and **IAM Policy** (JSON documents defining allow or deny rules).
  * Maximized root account security by successfully enabling **MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication)** using a mobile authenticator app.
  * Created an administrative **IAM User** for daily operations and verified its access, ensuring the Root Account is not used for everyday tasks in line with AWS security best practices.
