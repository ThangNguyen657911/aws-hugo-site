### General Assessment

**1. Work Environment**  
The work environment is very friendly and open. Members of FCAJ are always willing to support whenever I encounter difficulties, even outside of regular working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, helping me focus better. However, I think adding a few social events or team bonding activities would help everyone get to know each other better.

**2. Mentor / Admin Team Support**  
The mentor provided very detailed guidance, explained clearly whenever I didn't understand, and always encouraged me to ask questions. The admin team assisted with administrative procedures and documents, creating favorable conditions for me to work efficiently. I greatly appreciate that the mentor allowed me to try and resolve issues independently rather than just giving me the direct answers.

**3. Relevance Between Work and Major**  
The assigned work aligned well with the knowledge I acquired at university, while also expanding into new areas I hadn't been exposed to before. As a result, I was able to both reinforce my foundational knowledge and gain practical hands-on skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During my internship, I learned many new skills, such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, and professional communication in a corporate environment. My mentor also shared a lot of practical experience, which helped me better navigate my career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, works seriously, yet maintains a pleasant atmosphere. During urgent project phases, everyone works hard together and supports one another regardless of position. This made me feel like an integral part of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Intern Policies & Benefits**  
The company provides an internship allowance and offers flexible scheduling when needed. Additionally, being able to join internal training sessions is a major plus.
